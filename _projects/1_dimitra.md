**Dimitra** is a generative AI system for synthesizing audio-driven talking head videos with high temporal coherence and realistic lip-sync. Built during my time at [Inria Startup Studio](https://www.inria.fr/en/inria-startup-studio) (Centre at Université Côte d'Azur, France), the project combined research-grade diffusion transformer architecture with a production-ready product that was launched on Product Hunt.

**Core contributions:**
- Architected a diffusion transformer pipeline using cross-modal alignment to map audio signals to 3D facial latent embeddings, enabling expressive and temporally stable video synthesis.
- Optimized for real-time generation, enabling practical deployment in a presentation automation product where user-uploaded slides could be narrated by a photorealistic avatar.
- Led a 5-member cross-functional team through MVP launch, go-to-market strategy, and international showcases at VivaTech (Paris) and Bits & Pretzels (Munich).

The research component was published in the *International Journal of Computer Vision* (IJCV) — see [publications](/publications/) for details.
