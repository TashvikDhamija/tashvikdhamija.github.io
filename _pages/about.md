I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence and Innovation at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science. I work on generative AI, computer vision, and scalable deep learning systems. Currently an ML Intern at AWS Annapurna Labs, where I am enabling diffusion and flow-matching model training on Trainium hardware.

Before CMU, I co-founded an AI startup at Inria Startup Studio, building audio-driven video avatar generation systems showcased at VivaTech (Paris) and Bits & Pretzels (Munich). I have published in IJCV, IEEE T-BIOM, and Applied Intelligence, with work spanning video/image generation and detection, robot learning, and biometric restoration.
